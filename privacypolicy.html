Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to PawsnClaws. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of data when you use our services:

  • Your name, email, and phone number when signing up as a volunteer or adopter.
  • Information about pets you are interested in adopting or fostering.
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and usage patterns.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us improve our services, provide better support, and facilitate pet adoption and volunteer coordination.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no online system is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except as required by law.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies to improve user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to your data, correct inaccuracies, or request deletion.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at support@pawsnclaws.com.
